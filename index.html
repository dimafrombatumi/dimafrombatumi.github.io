Dolvo Safety and Child Protection Standards

Dolvo is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for any form of sexual content, exploitation, or abuse involving minors.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

Any material or behavior that promotes, depicts, or involves the sexual exploitation or abuse of children is strictly forbidden on the Dolvo platform. Violations may result in account suspension and reporting to relevant authorities.

Monitoring and Moderation

Dolvo actively monitors user activity to detect and remove harmful content. Reports of potential child exploitation are reviewed and prioritized immediately. We cooperate with law enforcement as required by law.

Reporting and Support

Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the Dolvo app. Our moderation team reviews each report and takes appropriate action to protect users and maintain platform integrity.
